# JST Connector Pinouts

Reference for common JST connector types used in projects.

## JST-XH (2.5mm pitch)

Common for battery connections and board-to-board.

## JST-PH (2.0mm pitch)

Smaller pitch, used in tighter spaces.

## JST-SH (1.0mm pitch)

Very small, used in compact sensors like Qwiic/STEMMA QT.
