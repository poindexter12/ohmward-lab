# Power Connectors

Reference for power connector types.

## Barrel Jacks

- 5.5x2.1mm - Most common for 5V/12V wall adapters
- 5.5x2.5mm - Less common variant

## USB

- USB-C - Modern, reversible, up to 100W with PD
- Micro-USB - Legacy, 5V only
- USB-A - Host side connector

## Screw Terminals

- Good for high current or frequent reconnection
- Common pitches: 2.54mm, 3.5mm, 5.0mm
